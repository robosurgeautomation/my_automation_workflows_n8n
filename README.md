# Automation Suite for Outreach

A collection of 6 specialized automation agents that work together to discover organizations, manage volunteers, and streamline communications for Muslim community initiatives.

## Agents Overview

### 1. Prospect Harvester
Finds UK businesses and organizations, extracts their contact information, and scores them based on data quality.

### 2. Volunteer Funnel Builder  
Automatically processes volunteer sign-ups from forms, saves their information, and sends welcome messages.

### 3. ISOC Scraper
Discovers UK University Islamic Societies, finds their websites and social media, and collects committee contact details.

### 4. Charity Scout
Searches newly registered UK charities and identifies Muslim/Islamic organizations with their complete contact information.

### 5. Cold Email Sequencer
Sends scheduled outreach emails to contacts while tracking responses and avoiding spam filters.

### 6. Prospect Harvester V2
Advanced version that finds organizations and their key decision-makers (CEO, Marketing, etc.) with AI verification.

## Purpose
These tools help automate community outreach, volunteer management, and partnership building for Muslim organizations - saving time while maintaining personal connections.
